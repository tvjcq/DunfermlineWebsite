DUNFERMLINE AT
A GLANCE

Dunfermline is a historic town in Scotland, located in the county of Fife. Once the country’s capital, it is known for its rich medieval heritage, including Dunfermline Abbey, the burial site of King Robert the Bruce. The town also played a key role during the Industrial Revolution and is the birthplace of Andrew Carnegie, the famous Scottish philanthropist. Today, Dunfermline is a vibrant town that blends history, culture, and green spaces, such as Pittencrieff Park.
